Welcome to SRM Buzz, where vision meets action. We prepare individuals to walk in purpose, steward their experiences wisely, and lead transformative lives through mentorship, leadership development, spiritual empowerment, and intentional impact.

Who We Are

Born from a divine burden to mentor through experience, SRM Buzz is more than a platform — it is a movement. Founded by Pastor E. Masaba, our work is grounded in faith, shaped by truth, and committed to raising leaders who influence society with wisdom and integrity.

Our Mission

To inspire, transform, and empower individuals to discover their God-given identity, live purposefully, and influence generations through truth and experiential mentorship.

Our Vision

To raise purpose-driven champions equipped through mentorship, spiritual empowerment, and intentional leadership development.
